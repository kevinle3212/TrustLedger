TrustLedger
Escrow for serious freelance work

Contracts drafted together. Funds protected on-chain.

TrustLedger combines encrypted contract drafting, optional live co-editing, EVM escrow, juror arbitration, reputation, and notifications into one open-source workflow for clients and freelancers.

1. Draft safely Encrypted links share terms first; live rooms are opt-in.
→
2. Fund escrow ETH or ERC-20 funds move through auditable contract paths.
→
3. Resolve fairly Approvals, warranties, ratings, and juror disputes stay traceable.
Encrypted collaboration Server relay stores ciphertext only. Keys stay with users.
Multi-chain foundation Sepolia-first EVM stack with Base, Optimism, Arbitrum config.
Strict engineering React Doctor, Jest, Playwright, Foundry, Docker, Kubernetes.
