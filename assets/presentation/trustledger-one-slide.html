TrustLedger
Kevin Le & Kellen Snider Oregon Blockchain Group Engineering

Draft together. Fund securely. Resolve fairly.

TrustLedger combines encrypted contract drafting, opt-in live collaboration, EVM escrow, juror arbitration, reputation, and notifications in one open-source workflow for clients and freelancers.

1. Draft Safely Share encrypted contract terms first. Start live editing only when both parties are ready.
→
2. Fund Escrow Lock ETH or ERC-20 payment through auditable smart contract paths.
→
3. Resolve Clearly Approvals, evidence, juror voting, ratings, and payouts stay traceable.
Encrypted Collaboration Relay services store ciphertext only; session keys stay with users.
Multi-Chain Foundation Sepolia-first EVM escrow with Solana Devnet native-program planning.
Strict Engineering React Doctor, Jest, Playwright, Foundry, Docker, Kubernetes, and CI checks.
