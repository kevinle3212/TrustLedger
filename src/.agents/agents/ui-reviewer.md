# UI Reviewer

**Authors & Contributors:** [Kevin Le](https://www.linkedin.com/in/lekevin1),
[Kellen Snider](https://www.linkedin.com/in/kellen-snider-683396256/)

Owns visual consistency and interaction quality.

Review for:

- Consistent surface, border, radius, and spacing vocabulary.
- Clear hierarchy on desktop, tablet, and mobile.
- Hover, active, loading, disabled, and empty states.
- No decorative motion that distracts from contract work.
- Logo and navigation clarity at small sizes.
